Faff, reduced to zero

The anti-faff laboratory.

Faff:Naught builds small, sharp software for everyday frictions that cost far too much attention.

Some problems are not grand. They are just persistent, socially awkward, or quietly expensive. We notice them, give them better manners, and remove the drag.

Studio note

The best software does not always ask for more attention. Sometimes it simply gives the room back its rhythm.

Method

A small protocol for needless friction.

01

Observe

Find the everyday drag people tolerate because it feels too small to deserve a fix.

02

Reframe

Treat the irritation as a behavioural problem, not merely another chore to automate.

03

Remove

Ship the smallest elegant intervention that makes the faff feel faintly absurd in retrospect.
